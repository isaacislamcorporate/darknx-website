COMPANY

Engineering the Future of AI Infrastructure.

DARKNX is a full-stack AI and HPC data center company. We design, build, and operate high-density infrastructure — from securing utility power and constructing facilities to manufacturing the components that go inside them.

Where most infrastructure companies rely on a chain of third parties, DARKNX controls the critical path end-to-end — enabling faster delivery, tighter specifications, and infrastructure that performs at the densities AI demands.

WHAT WE DO

MANUFACTURE
