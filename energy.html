ENERGY

From Energy to AI, It's All Important.

AI infrastructure at scale demands secured, redundant power from the utility source through to every rack. Drawing on deep experience in manufacturing power distribution equipment and deploying electrical grid infrastructure across multiple regions, DARKNX understands the complexities of securing utility power as well as integrating self-generation through solar, wind, thermal, natural gas, and other energy sources. Our team designs and builds the complete power delivery chain — from substations and generation through distribution and UPS systems — ensuring resilient, scalable energy for next-generation AI data centers.

UTILITY-FIRST APPROACH

DARKNX secures utility power as the foundation of every project — negotiating grid connections, substation agreements, and capacity allocations before construction begins, eliminating the most common cause of project delay.

CUSTOM POWER TOPOLOGY

Power topology is designed specifically for the project's load profile, redundancy requirements, and future expansion — not taken from a standard template and adapted after the fact.

IN-HOUSE POWER EQUIPMENT

DARKNX manufactures transformers, switchgear, ATS, and UPS systems in-house — removing external vendor dependencies from the most critical items on the power delivery critical path.
