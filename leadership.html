FOUNDERS

The People Behind the Build.

Isaac Islam
CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER & CHAIRMAN

Isaac Islam is the Chief Executive Officer of DARKNX, responsible for corporate strategy, capital partnerships, and the development of large-scale digital infrastructure platforms. He leads the structuring and execution of complex data center and energy infrastructure projects, working closely with institutional investors, developers, and technology partners to deliver scalable, high-performance compute environments across global markets.

Arman Salah
CTO & PRESIDENT, MANUFACTURING OPERATIONS

Arman Salah serves as Chief Technology Officer and President of Manufacturing Operations at DARKNX, overseeing engineering, infrastructure architecture, and industrial manufacturing across the company's data center and power systems portfolio. He leads the design and delivery of high-density AI infrastructure, advanced cooling systems, and integrated power platforms, enabling DARKNX to build and deploy next-generation data centers at scale.
